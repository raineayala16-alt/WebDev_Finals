Different Types of Strap

The strap of a Rolex watch is crafted from premium materials such as stainless steel, alligator leather, or ceramic. It offers a perfect blend of durability and elegance, designed to provide a comfortable fit while enhancing the watch’s luxurious appearance.

Different Types of Bezel

The bezel surrounds the watch face and can be made from materials like stainless steel, gold, or ceramic. It often features engraved markings, scales, or a smooth finish, serving both aesthetic and functional purposes, such as measuring elapsed time or providing a decorative frame.

Different Types of Markers

The indices are the hour markers on the dial, which can be simple batons, numerals, or luminous dots. They are designed to improve readability and add to the watch’s overall style, often coated with luminous material for visibility in darkness.
